Strategy:

The 3-Layer Marketing Stack

Every tool I build maps to one of three layers. Each layer builds on the one below it, so the models at the top are only as strong as the data at the bottom.

Layer 01

Data

In the age of AI, data is more than raw numbers â€” it's context. This layer ingests and structures inputs from CRM, analytics, and documentation to build a reliable foundation. Clean, contextualized data is what separates useful AI outputs from generic ones.

Layer 02

Intelligence

Intelligent insights drive decisions. This layer applies AI to connect the dots across your sources, surfacing patterns, opportunities, and recommendations rooted in data.

Layer 03

Channel

Insights are only valuable if they reach the right audience at the right time. This layer deploys the intelligence layer across the marketing funnel, from ad copy and email to SEO and content, each one grounded in real data and strategic context.
