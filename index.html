About

The Backstory

I spent years in marketing — running campaigns, managing attribution, and building reports that nobody trusted. The tools were either too simple or too enterprise. Nothing fit the messy middle where most marketing actually happens.

So I started building. Not another SaaS platform, but focused tools that solve one problem well. Each tool maps to a layer in what I call the 3-Layer Marketing Stack: Data, Intelligence, and Channel.

The URL Extractor is the first piece — the data layer. Because you can't optimize what you can't see, and you can't see what you haven't captured.

Layer 01

Data

Capture, extract, and structure the raw materials of marketing — URLs, parameters, campaign metadata.

Layer 02

Intelligence

Apply AI to transform raw data into actionable insights — pattern matching, anomaly detection, recommendations.

Layer 03

Channel

Deliver the right message through the right channel — automated, personalized, and measurable.
